# 🚲 Retail Bicycle Sales Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## 📌 Project Objective

The objective of this project was to analyze customer transaction data for a bicycle retail company and transform raw, disconnected Excel datasets into meaningful business insights. By cleaning, merging, and analyzing the data, an interactive Excel dashboard was developed to help management understand customer behavior, identify top-performing products and regions, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📂 Dataset Used

The project utilizes three interconnected <a href="https://github.com/ruhi1326/Retail_Bicycle_Dashboard/blob/main/Retail_Bicycle_Dataset.xlsx">Excel datasets</a>:

- Transaction Data
- Customer Demographic Data
- Customer Address Data

These datasets were merged into a single **Master Table** using **XLOOKUP**, enabling comprehensive sales and customer analysis.

---

## ❓ Business Questions (KPIs)

- What is the Total Revenue generated?
- What is the Total Profit earned?
- How many Total Transactions were completed?
- How many Unique Customers made purchases?
- Which state generates the highest revenue?
- Which customer wealth segment contributes the most revenue?
- Which bicycle brand drives the highest sales?
- Which age group contributes the highest revenue?
- How does revenue vary across different states?
- What is the monthly revenue trend?
- Which industry category generates the highest revenue?
- How does revenue differ between male and female customers?

## 📊 Dashboard

The interactive Excel dashboard includes the following KPIs:

- **Total Revenue**
- **Total Customers**
- **Average Order Value**
- **Top State**
- **Top Brand**
- **Top Wealth Segment**
- **Top Industry Category**

Interactive slicers were added for:

- State
- Wealth Segment
- Gender

---

## ⚙️ Process

- Explored and cleaned customer transaction, demographic, and address datasets.
- Removed inconsistencies in gender values, standardized state names, and corrected date formats.
- Merged multiple datasets into a single **Master Table** using **XLOOKUP**.
- Created calculated columns including Profit, Customer Full Name, Customer Age, Age Group, Month Name, and Year.
- Solved business questions using Excel functions such as **SUMIFS**, **COUNTIFS**, and **AVERAGEIFS**.
- Built Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts for business analysis.
- Designed an interactive Excel dashboard with KPI cards, charts, and slicers to visualize business performance.

---

## 💡 Project Insights

- The **Mass Customer** segment generates the highest revenue, making it the most valuable customer group.
- Customers aged **41–50 years** contribute the highest sales, representing the most profitable age segment.
- **NSW** is the top-performing state in terms of revenue generation.
- **Solex Bicycles** is the highest revenue-generating bicycle brand.
- Revenue peaks during **August** and **October**, indicating seasonal buying trends.

---

## ✅ Final Conclusion

The Retail Bicycle Dashboard provides valuable insights into customer purchasing behavior, regional sales performance, and product profitability through an interactive Excel dashboard.

Based on the analysis:

- Focus marketing campaigns and loyalty programs on the **Mass Customer** segment.
- Expand inventory and promotional activities in **NSW** to capitalize on strong regional demand.
- Strengthen partnerships with top-performing brands such as **Solex**, **WeareA2B**, and **Giant Bicycles**.
- Introduce premium bicycle models and exclusive offers for **High Net Worth** and **Affluent** customers.
- Plan seasonal marketing campaigns during peak sales months to maximize revenue.
